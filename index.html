About Me

As an innovator in Space Biotechnology and Synthetic Biology, my focus is strategic research in space biotechnology and radiobiology. With a background in biomedical engineering, I develop solutions for terrestrial applications and space exploration, translating complex discoveries (including multi-omics research, microbiome studies, astrobiology, and radiation tolerance) into tangible breakthroughs.

Main Affiliations

  • Space Radiobiology PhD Researcher, SNOLAB
  • Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer (CTO), Stealth Mode (Biotech)
  • STEAM Educator, AulaSPUTNIK
  • Communications Coordinator, NASA OSDR Microbes AWG

Collaboration & Open Science

Passionate about collaborative discovery and open science, I actively contribute to global initiatives like NASA OSDR GeneLab, Deep Space Initiative, and the iGEM Competition, aiming to accelerate advancements in space and synthetic biology and multi-omics for the benefit of all.

STEAM Education & Mentorship

My dedication extends to STEAM education, where I mentor future scientists through programs like AulaSPUTNIK and iGEM, creating engaging experiences to make complex science accessible and inspiring.
